Description

Ilusis is a gaming studio located at Belo Horizonte, Brazil. It was founded in 2008 and develops casual and mid-core games for mobile and portable platforms such as Sony PSP/Vita/PS3, Apple iPhone/iPad, Android and also for social media platforms such as Facebook.

History

Beginnings

The studio beginning traces back to a fish racing game conceived in 2006 as an university programming assignment. Upon graduation, Leonardo Arantes and Wallace Lages, decided to improve the game and submitted a prototype to a Brazilian game contest. Leonardo was in charge of the art and Wallace the programming. They won the contest and the money allowed them to rent office space and develop a PC version of the game.

Growing

In 2008 the team grew with the addition of three more partners: Alessandro Silva as CTO, Frederico Moura as CFO, and Rodrigo Mamão as CEO. In 2009 the studio received a major investment from the Brazilian Innovation Agency (FINEP) and won another game development contest. A year later, the studio joined the Sony Developer Incubation Program, which allowed them access to PlayStation development hardware and to the Sony Developer Support resources.

In 2012 Ilusis published Jett Tailfin Racers on Tegra Zone, Google Play and LG Store. In that year, the studio also released their first free-to-play game, Skyrise Runner. It was published on Apple Store, Google Play, Amazon Appstore and Intel AppUp, with good reviews.

Recent developments

In 2013 Ilusis received its first venture capital round. It enabled the studio to grow and improve its operations. In the same year it secured a partnership with the Latin America branch of Square Enix to develop SOCCER10, a social mobile game. Even though the publishing contract was rescinded mid-production, Ilusis decided to go forward with it. In 2014 the studio released its first console title: StreetKix Freestyle for PSP and PS Vita. It was also the first result of the Sony Latin America Incubation Program. Besides SOCCER10, Ilusis recently announced that it is working on another PS Vita game named KRINKLE CRUSHERS.

Ilusis is based at Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais – Brazil. (GMT – 3:00)
