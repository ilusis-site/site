Win the Blessed Fairy on Skyrise Runner!

by ilusis | Sep 13, 2012 | Blog | 0 comments

We have great news to you! On our new version of Skyrise Runner released on Google Play and App Store you can purchase the Blessed Fairy for free. As a consequence, you can bring Ciellu back to life without needing to gather coins for that. How it works? It’s very simple:

1- Go to the Power-ups screen.

2- Find the Blessed Fairy among the consumable items.

3- Click on the Facebook icon and like Ilusis Interactive Graphics’ Fan Page.

4- You will automatically receive the Blessed Fairy.

Enjoy!
