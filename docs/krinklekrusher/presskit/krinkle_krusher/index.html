Description

Krinkle Krusher is a new type of fantasy castle defense currently being developed for PS Vita, Playstation 3 and Playstation 4. Inspired by many tower defense games, Krinkle Krusher changes the player role granting him the chance to cast spells to defend you kingdom from the funny but dangerous creatures called krinkles. Krinkle Krusher will take you in an amazing history filled with many jokes, funny moments and a lot of spell casting!
