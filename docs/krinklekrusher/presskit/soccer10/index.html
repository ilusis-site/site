Description

SocceR10 is a game that brings to you the challenges and excitement of being a talented soccer player. When not playing matches you must work hard to improve your physical attributes and increase your Focus. These qualities are essential to succeed in the tournaments and advance your career. The game combines classic harvesting mechanics with an intuitive match system.

History

Welcome to SocceR10! Start your career in a trial match, prove that you are an amazing player to Ronaldinho and he will help you improve your skills. Ronaldinho isn't just a famous face in the game, he will help you to get hired in your first soccer club, where you will be able to improve your skills by creating training sites and upgrading your own talents. Once you prove that you are a great player, Ronaldinho will offer you the chance to play with him in his Dream Team, allowing you to experience where each player will be able to create or join Dream Teams and play against other players to show who's the best team in SocceR10.

Features

  • In SocceR10 you are the rising star of new soccer club. Your main concern is to improve your abilities and performance in the matches by training hard and playing matches. You can unlock and build several different drills depending on your level up strategy.
  • The game features an intuitive team and character customization system. You can decide how you will look like and choose a team crest and colors.
  • Along the game you can learn tricks with Ronaldinho, one of the best soccer players in the world. These tricks can be used during the matches to elude your adversaries. Special Focus power-ups can also be used to improve your chances.
  • SocceR10 also features a special Dream Team mode. Once unlocked you can invite your best friends to gather and dispute elite tournaments and win serious rewards!

About Vlambeer

Boilerplate
Vlambeer is a Dutch independent game studio made up of Rami Ismail and Jan Willem Nijman, bringing back arcade games since 1976. Vlambeer is best known for Super Crate Box, Radical Fishing, Serious Sam: The Random Encounter and GUN GODZ, even though they've released quite a few more games and experiments since their founding in September 2010.
