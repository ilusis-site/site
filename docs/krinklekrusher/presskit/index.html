Description

ILUSIS INTERACTIVE GRAPHICS is a Brazilian-based game development studio. Founded in 2008 by experts in the fields of computer graphics, design and engineering, Ilusis is a leading game developer in Brazil for downloadable (Mobile and handheld devices) platforms. The company stands out for its portfolio of projects and titles for multiple platforms (Android, iOS, Sony and Nintendo consoles). Our mission is to create fun content and spread it around the globe. We have grown over the last years as one of the most recognized studios in Latin America.

History

Ilusis was born from the passion for games, especially the ones for consoles and PC. At that time game development for console on Latin America was rough and soon the company realized the need to diversify, which lead to developing games for other companies and subsequently advergames, serious games and apps.

With the rise of mobile gaming, we also released some good games for iOS and Android like Skyrise Runner, Jett Tailfin Racers and SocceR10, this one our latest mobile game that is a mix of soccer and strategy genres.

However, the desire to make console games persisted in everyone at the studio.

A few months later, we released StreetKix for the Sony PSP and Jett Tailfin for the Nintendo WiiU, two games that helped us to learn a lot about developing for these platforms. Furthermore, as our support from Sony becomes stronger, we felt more confident that we could focus on developing great console games.

Currently we are working on Krinkle Krusher, our first completely independent game for the consoles PS3, PS4 and PSVita.
