About

It's a celebration day in the kingdom! The party has already started and every villager has the chance to eat the marvellous cake made from the fruit of the ancient magical tree!

However, no good comes without a problem, the cake’s scent has awakened an army of dangerous toothy creatures and now the Mage and his living glove have to save the day!

If you are hungry for some spell-casting castle defense action, Krinkle Krusher is your piece of cake!
